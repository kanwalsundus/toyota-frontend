The Lexus Group company profile.
Lexus is a division of Japanese automotive giant Toyota Motor Corporation that dedicates itself exclusively to making luxury cars.
Although initially the company used to manufacture only sedans and SUVs, Lexus has now branched further out and creates crossovers,
high performance cars, and hybrid models. The performance sub-division of Lexus now markets cars under the F-marque, such as the brilliant
Lexus LFA. ...


Lexus Sales and Special Offers
Lexus has reported sales in the United States market of 28,902 units in the month of July this year. This number represented an increase
of 3.63% on a volume basis from the 27,890 units that were sold during July of last year.

Despite the fact that the company’s sedans and coupes did not perform well in terms of sales in July 2017 (having posted a drop of 4.5%
compared to July 2016), the crossovers and SUVs of Lexus more than made up for it by climbing almost 18% to push the overall July sales of
the company into the green. Apart from the many leasing plans and financing options, buyers can also apply for credit through Lexus
Financial Services.
